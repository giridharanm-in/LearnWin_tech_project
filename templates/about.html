Our Story

Empowering Tech Careers Since 2012

Besant Technologies was founded with a single vision: make world-class IT education accessible to every aspiring student in India, completely free of cost.

Over the decade, we have trained more than 50,000 students across Chennai and beyond, helping them land jobs at top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, and many more.

Our curriculum is designed by industry practitioners and updated every quarter to reflect real-world demands — so you learn what employers actually need.
